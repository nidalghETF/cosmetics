1. Executive Summary

The Lebanese online cosmetics market presents a high-growth Direct-to-Consumer (D2C) opportunity. With COGS reduced to 15%, break-even accelerates to Months 6–8 and seed funding drops to $22–25K. Improved margins allow aggressive marketing, premium positioning, and faster market capture. The model is resilient against higher acquisition costs while maintaining profitability.
