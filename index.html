4. Competitive Landscape

Top online sellers in Lebanon include L’Oréal LB, Maybelline Leb, KIKO Lebanon, Bourjois LB, Lamcy Lebanon, Faces Lebanon, Riva Lebanon, Huda-mini, Miss-Beauty, Golden-Scent. Instagram is dominant (~85% of social traffic). COD is widely used (~82%), returns ~11–12%.

5. Import & Pricing Intelligence

Landed cost example for skincare: CIF $12.5 + Duty $1.88 + VAT $1.66 + Handling $0.35 + Last-mile $1.2 → $17.59/kg.

6. Consumer Personas & Search Trends

Top searches: skincare, perfumes, natural/organic, anti-aging, halal cosmetics. Fastest rising: “كريم اساس سائل طويل الامد” +480% YoY. Mobile share: ~78–87%. Peaks: June–July and Dec.

7. Regulatory & Payment

Payment gateways: MontyPay, Bank Audi, BLOM, Byblos, MTP. MDR: 2.9–4.2%, settlement T+1 to T+3. COD dominates ~82%. Regulatory: MOPH approvals, BDL compliance, banned/restricted ingredients.

9. Implementation Roadmap

  • Phase 1: Months 1–3: Aggressive CAC testing, premium packaging, direct supplier sourcing.
  • Phase 2: Months 4–6: Scale acquisition, enhance loyalty/subscription/referral programs.
  • Phase 3: Months 7–12: Competitive campaigns, influencer partnerships, content marketing.

10. Risk Mitigation

High margin absorbs CPL spikes. Margin advantage supports pricing resilience. Economic volatility mitigated by direct sourcing. Key KPIs: CPL < $8.50, LTV > $180, Burn < $4K, Gross Margin Retention > 68%.

11. Conclusion & Next Steps

  • Secure $22–25K seed funding
  • Begin platform development
  • Lock supplier cost advantage
  • Leverage margin for market entry, premium positioning, early regional expansion
  • Weekly P&L monitoring, flexible adaptation based on KPIs
